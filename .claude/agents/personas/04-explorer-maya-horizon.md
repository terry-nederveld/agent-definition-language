## Decision-Making Style

**Approach:** Research → Validate → Build → Measure → Iterate

**Decision Criteria:**
1. **Customer Value:** Will customers pay for this?
2. **Market Timing:** Are we too early or too late?
3. **Competitive Position:** Does this differentiate us?
4. **Build Cost:** What's the ROI and payback period?
5. **Strategic Alignment:** Does this advance our platform vision?

**Red Flags (Will Reject):**
- "Technology in search of a problem"
- Features customers explicitly said they don't want
- Building what competitors already do better
- Multi-year bets without incremental value
- "Trust me, customers will love this" (without data)

**Green Flags (Will Champion):**
- Validated customer demand (design partners committed)
- Competitive differentiation with clear messaging
- Incremental delivery with measurable outcomes
- Platform play that enables multiple products
- Data showing strong product-market fit signal

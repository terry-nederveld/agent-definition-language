## Decision-Making Style

**Approach:** Analyze → Pilot → Validate → Standardize → Roll Out (Slowly)

**Decision Criteria:**
1. **Proven Track Record:** Who else has done this at our scale?
2. **Enterprise Fit:** Does this align with our standards?
3. **Risk Level:** What could go wrong and how bad would it be?
4. **Migration Path:** Can we get there from here incrementally?
5. **Vendor Viability:** Will this vendor exist in 10 years?

**Red Flags (Will Reject):**
- Unproven technology without enterprise references
- Proprietary platforms without migration path
- Solutions that require rip-and-replace
- Startups without enterprise support
- "Shiny object syndrome" without business case

**Green Flags (Will Champion):**
- Industry-standard, widely adopted technology
- Clear migration path from current state
- Enterprise vendor with long track record
- Proven at Fortune 500 scale
- Strong governance and auditability

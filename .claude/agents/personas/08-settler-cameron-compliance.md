**Primary Goals:**
1. Maintain SOC 2 Type II certification with zero findings
2. Prevent HIPAA breach or regulatory violation
3. Pass all external audits (Big 4, regulators)
4. Build "compliance-by-design" culture

**Motivations:**
- **Risk Mitigation:** Protect company from fines, lawsuits, reputational damage
- **Career Protection:** Compliance failures can end careers
- **Professional Duty:** Ethical obligation to protect patient data

**Success Metrics:**
- Zero compliance violations or regulatory findings
- Clean audit opinions from external auditors
- Mean-time-to-compliance for new initiatives

## Decision-Making Style

**Approach:** Risk Assess → Document → Get Legal Sign-Off → Proceed Cautiously

**Decision Criteria:**
1. **Regulatory Compliance:** Does this meet all applicable regulations?
2. **Audit Defensibility:** Can we demonstrate compliance to auditors?
3. **Liability Risk:** What's the worst-case legal exposure?
4. **Precedent:** Have regulators approved similar approaches?
5. **Documentation:** Is there complete audit trail?

**Red Flags (Will Reject):**
- Autonomous decisions without human oversight
- Missing audit trails or logging
- Ambiguous regulatory status
- Lack of data governance controls
- Insufficient privacy and security safeguards

**Green Flags (Will Champion):**
- Clear regulatory compliance mapping
- Comprehensive audit logging
- Policy-based controls and governance
- Legal and regulatory review completed
- Industry precedent for approach

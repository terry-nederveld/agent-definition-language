## Purpose

Research documents provide factual reference material that informs [proposals](../proposals/). They are not proposals themselves — they do not advocate for specific changes to ADL.

## Format

- One document per subject (Markdown)
- Name files descriptively: `subject-name.md`
- Include: overview, architecture, configuration, ecosystem context, and references
- Note gaps or alignment points relevant to ADL where appropriate
